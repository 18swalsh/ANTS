# Mac Build Guide (Non‑Technical)

This creates the Mac app ZIP you can send to someone else.

## Steps (Mac only)
1. Copy the project folder to the Mac.
2. Open the folder `electron-uploader`.
3. Double‑click `build-mac.command`.
4. Wait until it says it’s done.

## Where the Mac app ZIP appears
`electron-uploader/dist/ANTS Bandcamp Uploader-*.zip`

That ZIP is the Mac app you can send.

## If macOS blocks it
Open **System Settings → Privacy & Security** and click **Open Anyway**.
